Make a cup of refreshing 'Dudh Chaa'

  • Water
  • Tea leaf
  • Cardamom
  • Milk powder
  • Sugar
  1. Boil some water in a pan
  2. Add crashed cardamom and tea leaf
  3. Boil on low flame for another 5-10 minutes
  4. Stain the tea
  5. Add milk powder and sugar
  6. Stir well
  7. Serve hot

Enjoy an amazing cup of dudh chaa!!!
